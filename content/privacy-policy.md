## Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 27, 2026**

FL Fence & Screen ("Company," "we," "us," "our") operates the flfenceandscreen.com website and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal information when you visit our website and use our services.

### 1. Information We Collect

We collect information from you in the following ways:

**Information You Voluntarily Provide:**
- Contact Form: When you submit our contact form to request an estimate, we collect your name, email address, phone number, the service you're interested in, and any project details you provide in the message field.
- Email Communication: If you email us directly at contact@flfenceandscreen.com, we collect your email address, name, and any information contained in your message.
- Phone Inquiries: If you call us at (386) 286-3262, we may collect your phone number and any information discussed during the call.

**Information Collected Automatically:**
- Analytics: We use Google Analytics to track website traffic, user behavior, and engagement metrics. This includes your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring website.
- Cookies: Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and track basic usage patterns. (See our Cookie Policy for details.)
- Server Logs: Our web hosting provider (Netlify) automatically collects server logs including IP addresses, request types, and timestamps.

### 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **Responding to Inquiries:** To respond to your estimate requests, answer questions, and provide information about our services.
- **Project Communication:** To communicate with you regarding estimates, scheduling, project updates, and service delivery.
- **Improving Our Website:** To analyze website performance, user experience, and service improvements.
- **Marketing Communication:** To send you occasional updates about our services (only if you opt-in or have an existing relationship with us).
- **Legal Compliance:** To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
- **Business Operations:** To manage our business, prevent fraud, and ensure website security.

### 3. Data Protection & Security

We take your privacy seriously and implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your information is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel who have agreed to maintain its confidentiality.

### 4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

**We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.**

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

- **Service Providers:** We may share necessary information with contractors, consultants, and service providers who assist us in operating our business and responding to your requests (e.g., email service providers).
- **Legal Compliance:** We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.
- **Netlify Hosting:** Your data may be processed by Netlify, our web hosting provider, in accordance with their privacy practices and data protection agreements.

### 5. Google Analytics

FL Fence & Screen uses Google Analytics to track website usage and performance. Google Analytics collects information such as:
- Your IP address
- Browser and device information
- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- Referring website and traffic source
- Geographic location

Google Analytics stores this information on Google's servers and may use it for their own purposes in accordance with their privacy policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on available at https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

### 6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies, which are small files stored on your device. These cookies help us:
- Remember your preferences
- Track website usage and performance
- Enhance your browsing experience
- Understand how visitors use our site

**Types of Cookies:**
- **Essential Cookies:** Required for basic website functionality (Netlify hosting).
- **Analytics Cookies:** Google Analytics cookies for tracking website performance.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when a cookie is being sent. However, blocking cookies may affect your website experience.

### 7. Your Rights

**Access & Correction:** You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to request corrections if any information is inaccurate.

**Deletion:** You have the right to request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal obligations to retain certain data.

**Opt-Out:** You can opt out of marketing communications by unsubscribing from our emails or requesting removal from our contact list.

**Do Not Track:** Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature. FL Fence & Screen does not currently respond to Do Not Track signals.

### 8. Retention of Information

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law. Contact form information is typically retained for at least two years to maintain service records and warranty documentation.

### 9. Children's Privacy

FL Fence & Screen does not intentionally collect information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete such information. Parents or guardians who believe their child has submitted information to our site should contact us immediately.

### 10. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request information about the personal data we collect, the sources of that data, our business purposes for collecting it, and the third parties with whom we share it. You can submit a privacy request by emailing contact@flfenceandscreen.com with "California Privacy Request" in the subject line.

### 11. Florida Compliance

FL Fence & Screen complies with the Florida Information Protection Act of 2014 (Fla. Stat. § 501.171) and other applicable Florida privacy laws. We implement reasonable information security practices appropriate to the nature of the information collected.

### 12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the revised policy on our website and updating the effective date. Your continued use of our website following changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
