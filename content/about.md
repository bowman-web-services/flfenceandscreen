## About FL Fence & Screen

For over five decades, FL Fence & Screen has been the trusted choice for homeowners and business owners throughout Volusia and Flagler Counties seeking quality fence installation, screen enclosure work, and custom outdoor structures. What began as a passion for craftsmanship has grown into a reputation for excellence that spans generations of satisfied customers.

### Our Story

FL Fence & Screen was founded on a simple principle: deliver exceptional quality and honest service to every customer, every time. Our team brings over 50 years of combined experience in fence installation, screen enclosure construction, and outdoor structure design. We understand that your home is your most valuable asset, and we approach every project with the care and attention it deserves.

Located in Port Orange, Florida, we've built our business by serving our community with integrity and skill. We're not a large corporation—we're your neighbors, local business owners who understand the unique challenges of building and maintaining properties in Florida's climate. That local knowledge, combined with our professional expertise, means your project is handled by people who genuinely care about getting it right.

Everything we do at FL Fence & Screen is built on four core commitments:

**Quality Craftsmanship**
Your project deserves the best materials and workmanship available. We believe in keeping you informed every step of the way. From your initial consultation through project completion, you'll know exactly what to expect. Our detailed estimates, regular updates, and accessibility mean there are no surprises—only satisfied customers.

**Timely Completion**
We respect your time. Our efficient scheduling and professional crew coordination mean your project moves forward on schedule. We plan carefully and execute with precision, completing work on time without cutting corners.

**Customer Satisfaction**
Your satisfaction is our ultimate goal. We listen to your needs, address your concerns, and stand behind our work with comprehensive warranty coverage. Our reputation is built on the positive experiences of thousands of happy customers throughout Volusia and Flagler Counties.
