## Cookie Policy

**Effective Date: March 27, 2026**

This Cookie Policy explains how FL Fence & Screen uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website located at flfenceandscreen.com (the "Website"). Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit our Website. This policy describes what cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can control them.

### 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files with data that is stored on your device's browser or local storage. When you visit a website, the website server can write cookies to your device, and when you revisit the site, your browser sends the cookie back to the server. Cookies allow websites to:
- Remember your preferences and settings
- Understand how visitors use the site
- Enhance user experience and functionality
- Track website performance and usage patterns

Cookies are distinct from other tracking technologies such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage, though they serve similar purposes.

### 2. Types of Cookies We Use

FL Fence & Screen uses the following categories of cookies:

**Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary)**
- **Purpose:** Essential cookies are necessary for the Website to function properly. They enable basic features such as page navigation and access to secure areas.
- **Provider:** Netlify (our web hosting provider)
- **Examples:** Session identifiers, security tokens, load balancing
- **Duration:** Session-based (deleted when you close your browser)
- **Opt-Out:** You cannot disable essential cookies without compromising Website functionality.

**Analytics Cookies**
- **Purpose:** We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our Website. Analytics cookies track:
  - Pages you visit
  - Time spent on each page
  - Links clicked
  - Geographic location
  - Device type and browser information
  - Traffic sources and referrers
- **Provider:** Google Analytics (Google LLC)
- **Cookie Name:** `_ga`, `_gid`, `_gat`, and related identifiers
- **Duration:** Typically 2 years
- **Use:** To improve Website design, content, and functionality based on user behavior patterns
- **Data Sharing:** Google Analytics data is processed on Google's servers and may be used by Google for their own purposes in accordance with their privacy policy.
- **Opt-Out:** See Section 4 below for instructions to opt out of Google Analytics tracking.

**No Advertising or Marketing Cookies**
- FL Fence & Screen does not currently use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or third-party marketing cookies. We do not track you across other websites for advertising purposes.

### 3. How We Use Cookie Information

The information collected through cookies is used to:
- **Improve Website Performance:** Analyze traffic patterns and usage to optimize site speed, design, and user experience.
- **Understand User Behavior:** Identify which pages are most valuable and which may need improvement.
- **Enhance Security:** Detect and prevent fraudulent activity and unauthorized access.
- **Website Analytics:** Generate reports on visitor demographics, traffic sources, and engagement metrics.
- **Personalization:** Remember your preferences to enhance your experience on return visits (limited use).

We do not use cookies to personally identify you or to combine cookie data with other personally identifying information (such as your name or email address) without your explicit consent.

### 4. Your Cookie Choices & Controls

**Managing Cookies in Your Browser:**
Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through browser settings. You can:
- **Accept all cookies** — Allow all cookies to be set on your device
- **Block all cookies** — Prevent all cookies from being set on your device
- **Block third-party cookies** — Allow first-party cookies but block cookies from external domains
- **Delete cookies** — Remove previously stored cookies from your device

**Browser Instructions:**
- **Google Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and Other Site Data
- **Mozilla Firefox:** Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari (Mac/iOS):** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and Website Data
- **Microsoft Edge:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and Other Site Data
- **Internet Explorer:** Tools → Internet Options → Privacy → Cookies

**Important Note:** Blocking essential cookies may prevent the Website from functioning properly, including navigation, form submission, and security features. You may experience reduced functionality if you disable cookies.

### 5. Opting Out of Google Analytics

FL Fence & Screen uses Google Analytics to track Website usage. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by:

**Option 1: Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Extension**
- Download the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on from Google's official page: [https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- This extension prevents your data from being sent to Google Analytics
- Available for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge

**Option 2: Disable in Browser Settings**
- Many modern browsers include privacy settings that can limit or disable analytics tracking
- Check your browser's privacy and security settings

**Option 3: Opt-Out Links**
- Google provides an opt-out mechanism at [https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/181881](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/181881)

When you opt out of Google Analytics, your browser will send a signal that prevents data collection. This preference is browser and device-specific, so you will need to opt out on each device and browser you use.

### 6. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that sends a signal to websites indicating your preference not to be tracked. FL Fence & Screen currently does not respond to or honor Do Not Track browser signals, though we encourage you to use the cookie management and analytics opt-out options described above if you prefer to limit tracking.
