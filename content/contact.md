## What to Expect

When you contact FL Fence & Screen, here's what happens next:

1. **Initial Contact** — We'll respond to your inquiry promptly, typically within 24 business hours.
2. **Project Discussion** — We'll ask questions about your vision, budget, timeline, and specific needs.
3. **Site Visit** — For most projects, we'll schedule a time to assess your property in person.
4. **Detailed Estimate** — We'll provide a comprehensive, written estimate with materials, labor, and timeline.
5. **No Pressure** — Our estimate is free and non-binding. Take your time to decide.
6. **Next Steps** — Once you're ready, we'll schedule your project and get to work.

We look forward to helping you create the outdoor space of your dreams!
