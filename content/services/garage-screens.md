## Types of Garage Screens

**Sliding Garage Screens** mount on your existing garage door frame and glide left and right like curtains. They're the most popular option because they're easy to use, don't interfere with your door mechanism, and provide full coverage. You can open them in seconds for vehicle access, and they collapse completely to the side when not needed. Sliding screens work beautifully for daily use in homes across Volusia County.

**Roll-Up Garage Screens** operate like roller blinds—they roll up into a compact housing mounted at the top of the opening. Roll-up systems work well in tight spaces and look cleaner when retracted. They're slightly more expensive than sliding screens but offer excellent durability and a more finished appearance, especially in homes with custom garage aesthetics in Daytona Beach and upscale Port Orange neighborhoods.

**Fixed Garage Screens** are permanently mounted and don't retract. These work for garages you don't need vehicle access to frequently, or for bonus rooms and covered spaces where you want permanent bug protection without the ability to remove the screen. They're the most affordable option and the most durable.

**Motorized Garage Screens** use a motor to open and close with a remote control or wall button. Perfect for Daytona Beach homes where convenience matters, motorized screens work with your existing garage door opener. They're especially popular for pool-side garages, outdoor kitchens, or integrated outdoor living spaces throughout Volusia County.
