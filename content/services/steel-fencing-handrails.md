## Materials and Fabrication

**Steel Profiles**: Angle iron, channel, square tubing, and specialty profiles selected for structural requirements and aesthetic goals.

**Welding**: Professional welding ensures structural integrity. All welds are stress-relieved and inspected for quality.

**Surface Preparation**: Proper preparation—grinding, cleaning, sandblasting—ensures paint adhesion and longevity.

**Finish Options**:
- **Powder Coating**: Factory-applied finish in numerous colors providing durability and aesthetic appeal. Most common choice for quality installations.
- **Hot-Dip Galvanizing**: Zinc coating providing corrosion resistance. Especially important for coastal Volusia County properties.

- **Stainless Steel**: Premium option for maximum corrosion resistance in coastal environments.

## Coastal Considerations

For Daytona Beach, New Smyrna Beach, Ponce Inlet, and Edgewater properties, steel requires special consideration:

**Corrosion Prevention**: Hot-dip galvanizing or premium powder coating is essential for steel longevity in salt-air environments.

**Maintenance**: Annual inspection and touching-up of coating prevents rust initiation.

**Stainless Steel Options**: For maximum corrosion resistance, stainless steel is worth the premium cost in coastal locations.

## Maintenance Requirements

Steel maintenance varies by finish:

**Powder-Coated Steel**: Minimal maintenance. Annual cleaning with mild soap and water. Inspect for coating damage; touch up if needed.

**Galvanized Steel**: Inspect annually for rust. Touch up immediately if corrosion appears. More maintenance than powder-coated but more economical.

**Stainless Steel**: Virtually maintenance-free. Occasional cleaning maintains appearance. No rust possible.
